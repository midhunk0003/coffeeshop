What make Our Coffe Special?

All coffees are categorized into two- commodity and specialty. Commodity coffee is just traded to get the job done. You often have no idea where it is grown,

how it affects your environment and it just tastes like ‘coffee’.

The commodity game is all about quantity but specialty coffee is all about quality. All coffees are graded on a 100 point scale. For a coffee to be considered ‘specialty’ it must score at least an 80.
